BeepBeepy Privacy Policy

The BeepBeepy app (the "App") is provided by Luis Quesada Torres ("the Author"). This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

1. No Data Collection

The App is designed to be privacy-respecting.

  • Personal Data: The Author does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, address, or location).
  • Device Data: The App does not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or technical telemetry.
  • Offline Operation: The App operates entirely locally on your device and does not require an internet connection to perform its core functions.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party service providers (such as advertising networks, analytics, or crash reporting SDKs) that could track your activity or collect data about you.

3. Permissions

Any permissions requested by the App on your Android device are used strictly for the local functionality of the notification features. No data accessed via these permissions is ever uploaded, shared, or stored off the device.

4. Changes to This Policy

The Author may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document and pushing the changes to the GitHub repository.

5. Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy, please open an Issue on the official GitHub repository.
